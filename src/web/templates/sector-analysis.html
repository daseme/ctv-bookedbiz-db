Sector Analysis Report

What is this report?

Shows revenue distribution across sector groups for a selected year. Sectors are assigned to customers via the Sector Manager and roll up into groups: Outreach, Commercial, Political, and Other.

Sector Groups

Each sector belongs to a group. The cards show total revenue, spot count, and individual sector breakdown within each group. Percentages are relative to the group total.

Top Customers

The table at the bottom lists the highest-revenue customers for the selected year, along with their sector, spot count, and average spot rate.

Year Filter

Use the dropdown to switch between years. Revenue is based on broadcast month, so a year includes all spots whose broadcast month falls within that calendar year.

Revenue Exclusions

Trade revenue is excluded from all figures. Only cash spots contribute to the totals.

Unassigned Customers

Customers without a sector appear in the "Other" group. Use the Sector Manager to assign them.
