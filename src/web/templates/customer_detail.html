Customer Detail Report

Monthly Revenue Chart

Shows gross revenue by broadcast month for the most recent 24 months that have activity. Only months where this customer had spots appear — gaps between bars mean the customer had no spots that month.

KPI Cards

Without a date filter: lifetime totals, current-year YTD with year-over-year change, prior full-year totals, and average spot rate across all time. With a date filter active: totals and averages are scoped to the filtered range and the YoY cards are hidden.

Date Filter

Use the date picker bar at the top to narrow every section on this page — KPIs, chart, breakdowns, and recent activity — to a specific air-date range. Click Reset to return to the lifetime view.

Revenue Exclusions

Trade revenue is excluded from all calculations on this page. Only cash spots contribute to gross and net figures.

Breakdowns

Language, Account Executive, and Market sections show each category's share of total gross revenue. Percentages and bars are relative to the current view (lifetime or filtered range).

Bill Code Aliases

The raw billing codes from traffic logs that resolve to this customer. When new spots arrive with one of these codes, they are automatically linked to this customer record.
