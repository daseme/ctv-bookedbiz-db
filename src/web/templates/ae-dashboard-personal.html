My Dashboard — Quick Guide

What Is This Page?

My Dashboard is your personal revenue command center. It tracks your booked revenue against budget and forecast month by month, shows recent contract activity, and flags operational items like missing copy and pending insertion orders.

Waiting for Copy

Spots that have been booked but still need creative copy assigned. These show up when the “media” field on a spot contains “NEED COPY.” Each row shows the customer, contract, month range, spot count, and gross revenue. Get copy submitted so these spots can go to air on schedule.

Pending Insertion Orders

Contracts that have been entered into the system but don’t yet have a signed insertion order on file. These need follow-up with the client or agency to get the paperwork completed.

Contracts Added (Last 7 Days)

A quick highlight reel of contracts booked in the past week. Today’s additions are highlighted in blue. Click “See More” for the full contracts-added report with additional detail and date filtering.

Summary Cards

Three cards showing your year-to-date position:

  • Total Booked Revenue — confirmed orders in the system, shown as a percentage of your annual budget
  • Total Forecast — your projected revenue (booked + expected), shown as a percentage of budget
  • Total Budget — your assigned annual revenue target, with booked-vs-forecast percentage showing how much of your pipeline has converted

Color coding: green = at or above target, gray = close, red = below target.

Monthly Revenue Chart

A line chart plotting your booked revenue, forecast, and budget month by month. Use it to spot trends — months where booked is tracking below forecast may need attention. Click “Show Details” to expand the full monthly breakdown table with exact numbers and percentages.

Monthly Breakdown Table

Detailed month-by-month numbers (toggled from the chart). Each row shows:

  • Booked Revenue — confirmed orders for that month
  • Forecast — your projected total (booked + expected)
  • Budget — your monthly target
  • Booked vs Forecast — how much of your pipeline has converted to orders
  • Forecast vs Budget — whether your pipeline is on track to hit budget
  • Booked vs Budget — the bottom line — are confirmed orders meeting the target?

Account Revenue

Every account in your book grouped by sector, with revenue and percentage of total budget. Click a sector row to expand and see individual accounts. Click an account name to go to its detail page.

  • Date filters — narrow the revenue window to a specific period
  • Sort Sectors — alphabetical or by revenue
  • Sort Accounts — by revenue or name within each sector

Use this to understand where your revenue is concentrated and which accounts are driving your numbers.
