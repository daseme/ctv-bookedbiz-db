Revenue Classification Manager

What is this?

This tool helps you understand revenue in two buckets: Regular (recurring, predictable revenue like casinos, auto dealers, healthcare) and Irregular (cyclical or one-time like political campaigns or COVID-era government spending).

Why it matters

The board needs to see the predictability story. Regular revenue is what we can count on year-over-year. Irregular revenue inflates totals during peak cycles but disappears in off-years. Separating them makes forecasting reliable.

Summary Cards

  • Regular Revenue -- Total booked revenue from regular customers for the selected year
  • Irregular Revenue -- Total booked revenue from irregular customers
  • Regular % -- What portion of total revenue is predictable
  • Unclassified -- Customers without a classification (should be zero)

Monthly Chart

Shows regular (blue) and irregular (amber) revenue side by side for each month. Includes forward-booked months, not just closed months.

Reclassifying Customers

Click the Regular or Irregular button in the table to toggle a customer's classification. Changes save immediately and update all totals.

Filters

Use the year selector, sector, AE, and classification dropdowns to focus on specific subsets. The search box filters by customer name.
